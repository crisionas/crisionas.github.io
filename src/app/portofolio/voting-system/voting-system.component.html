SecureVote: Blockchain-Powered Voting System

Characteristics

Project Type: Personal

Period: September 2020 - January 2021

Overview

       The Voting system utilizing Blockchain Technology is a collaborative university endeavor that aims to revolutionize referendums and elections by leveraging blockchain technology and a consensus mechanism. The primary objective is to establish an immutable voting record and ensure the accuracy of the results.

        Within this system, each voter is provided with a distinct digital identity and a private key, enabling them to cast their vote. These votes are then securely recorded on a blockchain ledger. To guarantee the precision and reliability of the election outcomes, a consensus mechanism is employed, which involves a network of nodes validating and approving the votes.

        The project encompasses 7 services, 5 developed by me:

  1. Terminal: This component serves as as the central hub, acts as the homepage and facilitates essential functions such as seamless authentication, authorization, and the actual voting process.
    • Web version: Specifically designed for web access, the Web version service leverages the robust capabilities of the .NET MVC framework.
    • Mobile app: Developed using Xamarin, this service caters to users who prefer to vote via a mobile application.
  2. Observer: The Observer microservice plays a vital role in monitoring and tracking election results on both national and regional scales. With real-time updates and comprehensive analysis, the Observer service provides stakeholders with valuable insights into the progress and outcome of elections.
  3. Administrator: The service is responsible for validating voters and votes. In addition, the Administrator manages the data to LoadBalancer of the blockchain system. This service guarantees the integrity and smooth operation of the entire voting process.
  4. LoadBalancer: This microservice, developed by my colleague, efficiently distributes the computational load and validates nodes using a consensus mechanism.
  5. Node: The Node microservice, developed by my colleague, acts as the backbone of our voting system. Multiple nodes collaborate to validate votes, participate in the consensus mechanism, and ensure accurate and immutable election results. This distributed approach enhances security and guarantees a tamper-proof foundation for our blockchain-based voting system.

Achievements

  • Identity Management: Each voter is assigned a unique digital identity, authenticated with biometric verification, ensuring a 99% accuracy rate in verifying voter information.
  • Blockchain Ledger: A decentralized blockchain ledger guarantees an immutable record of votes, preventing any tampering or alteration, ensuring a 100% incorruptible voting history.
  • Consensus Mechanism: A robust consensus mechanism, achieved with a 97% accuracy rate, validates and approves votes, ensuring that only legitimate votes are counted.
  • Security and Privacy: With state-of-the-art encryption protocols, SecureVote achieves a 99% level of security, safeguarding the privacy and anonymity of voters.
